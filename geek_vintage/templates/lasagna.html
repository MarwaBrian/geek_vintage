Lasagna

Prep Time- 15mins

Cook Time- 90mins

Serving- 8

For meat sauces:
  • 2 Tablespoons Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 Pound ground beef chuck
  • ½ medium onion, diced
  • ½ large paprika, sliced
  • 2 cloves and garlic, minced
  • 5 fresh large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 Tablespoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1 Tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • ¼ cup Sugar(For taste, optional)
  • Salt
Assemble lasagna:
  • ½ Pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces Ricotta Cheese
  • 1½ pounds Mozzarella cheese, grated or sliced
  • ¼ pound freshly grated parmesan cheese

Procedure
  1. Place a large pot of water on high heat.
  2. Add 1 Tablespoon salt for every 2 quarts of water and bring to boil
    3. (while waiting for it to boil, prepare the sauce)
  3. In a large skillet heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil on medium high heat, add the ground beef and cook until it is lightly browned on all sides, remove and drain most of the fat.
  4. Add the diced paprika and onions, then cook until the paprika softens and onions turn brown.
  5. Add the minced garlic and cloves, cook for half minute then add the browned beef and cook for 5mins with low heat.
  6. Transfer to a large pot, add the remaining sauce ingredients.
  7. Bring the sauce to simmer and maintain the heat on low for 15-45 mins. Then remove from heat.
    8. Tip:Scrape the bottom ever so often so nothing sticks to the bottom or scorches.

    The water should be boiling by now, so back to our pasta.

  8. Add the lasagna noodles and cook them per package directions.
  9. When ready, drain, rinse with cool water and gently separate any noodles sticking together.
  10. Spread a little olive oil on a large rimmed baking sheet and lay out the cooked noodles, tunong them over so that they get coated to prevent them sticking together.
  11. Heat the oven to 375°F.

    12. Assembling the lasagna.

  12. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in a casserole or laasagna dish (9x13-inch). Top with 3 lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, ⅓ cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese and 1 cup pasta sauce. Top with remaining lasagna noodles and remaining pasta sauce. Then sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  13. Cover with slighly tented aluminium foil and bake at 375°F for 45 minutes.
    14. Tip:Uncover for last ten minutes if you like more of a crusty top and edges.
  14. Remove from oven and allow to cool for about 15 minutes.
  15. Serve!!
